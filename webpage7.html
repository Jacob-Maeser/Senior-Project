Welcome to the Custom Software Page. Below there will be two separate forms to be filled out based on what fits you best. There will be an option for businesses and one for individuals. The forms are very similar, just be sure to fill out the right one so it is easier for our team to distinguish who the custom software is for.

After this is filled out and submitted, it will be sent to us and we will determine if we can approve the project at that time. Gaining approval will be based on whether you are clear about what you want, if we can develop it within your budget, and if we can get it done within your timeline. Before denying any proposal, we will contact you and see if you can give us more information, and /or be flexible. If approved, we will contact you about your approval and get a team together. Once the team is determined, we will begin designing, then developing, implementing, and testing. Throughout this process we will update the customer approximately once a week to go over what was accomplished. We may contact more than that if we have a question or something important comes up. Once we implement and test the customized software, we will also train you/the company how to use it.
